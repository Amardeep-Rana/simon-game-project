#HowToPlay
start
#Rules #1. Press the 'start' button to start the game. #2. Simon will play a sequence of colors by blinking its lights and playing a sound. #3. Repeat the sequence by pressing the colored buttons in the correct order. #4. Simon will add one more color to the sequence each time you play the sequence correctly. #5. The sequence will get longer and faster as you keep playing. #6. If you make a mistake, the game will end and your level will be displayed.
